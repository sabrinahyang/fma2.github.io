Wk 1 Cultural: Identifying Core Values

When I think of times in my life I’ve been the happiest, the proudest, or the most satisfied, I recall all of the following values:

FAIRNESS

The importance of this value to me stems, in part, from my years as a teacher in the Bronx. All of my students’ families were low-income, most below the poverty line. What motivated me everyday in working with my students was the desire to ensure they had/have access to the same opportunities I, a person who was just lucky to be born into privilege, was afforded growing up – I seeked fairness.
